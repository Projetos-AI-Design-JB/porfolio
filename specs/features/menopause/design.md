# Design System: Menopause Wellness
## Visual Identity
The visual identity is built on the concept of "Scientific Serenity." It combines the precision of functional medicine with the warmth of a supportive wellness sanctuary.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Sage)**: `#7D8F69` - Used for primary accents, success states, and calming backgrounds.
- **Background (Warm Cream)**: `#F9F7F2` - The canvas of the application, providing a soft, non-clinical feel.
- **Text (Charcoal)**: `#1A1A1A` - High contrast for readability and authority.
- **Accent (Muted Rose)**: `#D4A373` - Subtle warmth for CTA buttons and important highlights.

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Playfair Display` (Serif) - To convey elegance, sophistication, and a human touch.
- **Body**: `Outfit` (Sans-serif) - For optimal readability and a modern, scientific feel.

## UI Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded with generous padding. Soft shadows and subtle scale transitions.
- **Cards**: Minimalist with `border-white/20` and `bg-white/40` backdrop-blur effects.
- **Inputs**: Clean, outlined, and spacious.

## Motion & Interaction
- **Scroll Reveal**: Elements should fade in and move up slightly (`y: 20`) as they enter the viewport.
- **Smooth Scroll**: Lenis or standard smooth scroll integration.
- **Hover States**: Subtle opacity shifts or scale changes (`scale: 1.02`).

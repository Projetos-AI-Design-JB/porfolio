# Feature: Menopause Education & Empowerment
## Vision
Create a serene, trustworthy, and sophisticated digital sanctuary for women navigating menopause. The platform balances science-backed information with a calming wellness aesthetic.

## Target Audience
- Women aged 40-60.
- Seeking high-end, professional, and empathetic guidance.
- Prefers clean, minimalist, and easy-to-navigate interfaces.

## Key Pillars
1. **Calmness**: Soft colors, generous white space, smooth transitions.
2. **Authority**: Professional typography, clear hierarchy, high-quality imagery.
3. **Conversion**: Frictionless path to video purchase and "Work With Me" inquiries.

## Technical Requirements
- Next.js 14+ (App Router).
- Stripe Integration for video sales.
- Resend for email notifications.
- Responsive design (Mobile First).
- SEO Optimized structure.
